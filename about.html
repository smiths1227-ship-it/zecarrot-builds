who

i'm zecarrot. 11th grader, based in Texas. i'm into how things are built — like actually built, structurally, from the ground up. my main interest is architectural engineering, which is basically the overlap between architecture and structural engineering. i think about load paths and why buildings don't fall down more than a normal person probably should.

outside of school i build stuff — code projects, fabrication stuff in PAE class, games, simulations. if there's a problem worth solving i'll probably try to make something that solves it.

why gustave eiffel

the dude built the Eiffel Tower in 1889 with basically no computers. just math, iron, and the audacity to think it wouldn't fall over. it was supposed to be temporary and now it's one of the most recognizable structures on earth. that's insane. that's the kind of engineer i want to be.
